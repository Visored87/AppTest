Things To Keep In Mind(Graduate)

Test scores:

	- Check with your department to determine test score requirements. 
	** If admissions exams are required, submit your test scores to the department through the testing agency.


Money:

	- Apply for scholarships, assistantships and fellowships.

	- Check with your department for assistantship and fellowship applications.

	- Apply for financial aid at studentaid.gov. Priority date: Jan. 15

	- Priority date for other awards: Varies by program



Application status:

	- Check the status of your admission application through your my.unt.edu portal.
